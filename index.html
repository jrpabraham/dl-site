Our Story

David and Leyanne crossed paths in April 2017. David had a crush on her but it wasn't until January 2019 that the spark between them ignited into a flame of romance. Through the ups and downs, navigating the challenges of a global pandemic and conquering the trials of a long-distance relationship while David pursued his master's in Florida, their love only grew stronger.

David and Leyanne have shared countless moments of joy, laughter, and adventure. From exploring new destinations during little trips together to trying different foods at new restaurants, each experience added a unique touch to their love story. Through the years, they found joy in the simple pleasure of life. Whether it was embarking on new adventures or enjoying quiet times of togetherness, the moments they spent together became priceless memories they will both cherish in their hearts.

In November 2023, David asked Leyanne to be his forever, and with tears of joy and hearts full of love, they embraced the next chapter of their story—an engagement that marked the beginning of a lifetime together. Through marriace, they will continue to grow in love, aspire to travel the world and experience new adventures, and go on to chase their dreams together.
